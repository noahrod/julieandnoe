We joyfully invite you to join us as we celebrate the baptism of Vincent.

Vincent will be baptized on Sunday, July 23rd, 2023 at 12:00pm in the [Basilica of St. Josaphat](https://thebasilica.org/) at [2333 S 6th St, Milwaukee, WI 53215](https://goo.gl/maps/eEKbLFKUWARqecBE6?coh=178572&entry=tt). We would be honored to have you witness this special moment and share in our joy. The mass will start at noon, and the baptism at 1:00pm.
